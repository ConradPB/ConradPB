### 🏗️ Flagship Project: MilkWize 🥛
> **The Problem:** Rural farmers lack connectivity and climate context for milk production.  
> **The Solution:** A high-performance **Android app** that tracks yields and correlates them with real-time **Heat Stress (THI)** data.

**Key Technical Achievements:**
* **Real-Time Data Sync:** Engineered an offline-first engine with **Room/SQLite** and **Supabase WebSockets**.
* **Environmental Logic:** Integrated **Open-Meteo APIs** to calculate biological stress indicators on the fly.
* **Secure Reporting:** Implemented **Android FileProvider** for encrypted financial CSV sharing.
